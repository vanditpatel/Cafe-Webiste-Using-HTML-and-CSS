The Cafe
Cafe | Brasserie | Bar

The menu delights with authentic European cuisine, an all day breakfast and cocktails. Some of the menu specialties are akin to a fine dining restaurant but at value for money prices. It is also the perfect place to enjoy a nice Sunday brunch with food critics claiming we have "Lipsmacking Burgers"
